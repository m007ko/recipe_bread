  • Prep Time: 3 days
  • Cook time: 2 hour

Part 1 - Yeast Starter Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of flour (better fullcorn)
  • Patience and good mood.

Equipment

  • Large flask or jar (2 liters)
  • Spatula
  • Peace of clouth
  • Measuring cup

Steps

  1. Mix 1 cup of water with 1 cup of flour
  2. Let the starter rest for 24 hours at room temperature.
  3. Add 1 cup of water, mix, add 1 cup of flour, mix again.
  4. Repeat step 2 and 3 two times.
  5. Magic - your own starter is ready! Store it in frige and don't forget to feed every week.

Part 2 - Bread

Ingredients

  • 400 g of Starter (from Part 1)
  • 150 g of swiss water
  • 400 g of flour (I use mix)
  • 1 spoon of salt
  • (optional) seeds of your choice
  • (optional) malt - add color and taste
  • Patience and good mood

Equipment

  • Large boul
  • Spatula
  • Peace of clouth
  • Measuring cups
  • Oven

Steps

  1. Mix all ingridiens.
  2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Mix again and put all dough in baking form
